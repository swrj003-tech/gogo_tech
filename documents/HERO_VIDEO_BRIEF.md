# Premium Hero Video Brief

## Objective
Create an 8-second seamless loop video for the hero background that conveys "premium energy infrastructure" and "reliability".

## Specifications

- **Duration**: 8 seconds (exact loop)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Bitrate**: Low (max 2Mbps) for web optimization
- **Audio**: Muted (no audio track)
- **Style**: Cinematic, slow-motion, warm grading (matching #FED75F and #ED6A21)

## Scene Composition

1. **0:00 - 0:03**: Close-up of a premium, clean fuel nozzle connecting to a vehicle (implies precision).
2. **0:03 - 0:06**: Wide shot of a GoGo branded truck driving smoothly on a highway at sunset (implies motion/freedom).
3. **0:06 - 0:08**: Drone shot of a fleet yard with vehicles being serviced (implies scale).

## Technical Requirements

- **Color Grade**: Warm, golden hour tones. High contrast.
- **Overlay**: The video will have a 60% opacity black gradient overlay, so ensure highlights are bright.
- **Motion**: Slow, steady camera movements. No shaky cam.

## Delivery

1. **High-res Master**: ProRes 422
2. **Web Optimized**: MP4 (H.264), no audio, < 5MB file size.
